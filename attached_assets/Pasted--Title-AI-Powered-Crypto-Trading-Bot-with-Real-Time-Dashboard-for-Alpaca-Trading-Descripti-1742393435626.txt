
**Title:** AI-Powered Crypto Trading Bot with Real-Time Dashboard for Alpaca Trading  

**Description:**  
Develop an AI-driven trading bot that operates on **Alpaca Trading’s API**, leveraging **REST API** for market data retrieval and trade execution, and **WebSockets** for real-time updates. The bot should autonomously **analyze market trends**, execute trades, and optimize **portfolio performance** while maintaining a high **Sharpe Ratio**.  

### **Core Features:**  

#### **1. Real-Time Data & Trading Execution**  
✅ **Fetch live market data** via Alpaca's REST API & WebSocket  
✅ **Execute trades autonomously** based on AI-driven strategy  
✅ **Track portfolio value & positions in real-time**  

#### **2. AI Trading Strategy**  
✅ Supports **PPO, Reinforcement Learning (RL), Mean Reversion, Momentum-based strategies**, or custom AI logic  
✅ Ability to **backtest strategies** and adapt dynamically  
✅ Incorporate **technical indicators** (RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, etc.)  
✅ **Optimize risk-return profile** for high Sharpe Ratio  

#### **3. Interactive Dashboard**  
🎨 **Theme:** Modern **black-blue UI** with **real-time charts & stats**  
📊 **Live visualizations** of portfolio value, trade history, order book, P&L  
🔄 **WebSocket integration** for instant market & trade updates  
🎯 **Performance metrics:** Portfolio return, Sharpe Ratio, Win/Loss rate  

#### **4. Automated & Manual Control**  
⚡ **Start/Stop Trading** button for manual intervention  
⚙️ Adjustable **risk parameters & trading frequency**  
🔔 **Trade notifications/logs** on **dashboard & console**  

#### **5. Logging & Monitoring**  
📜 **Console logs of all trades & decisions**  
📡 **Error handling & fail-safe mechanisms**  
📊 **Historical trade data storage for analysis**  

---

**Tech Stack & Implementation Requirements:**  
✔ Python for bot logic & API communication  
✔ FastAPI/Flask for backend & WebSockets  
✔ React.jsfor interactive dashboard  
✔ Chart.js or Recharts for real-time graphs  


---

### **Expected Outcome:**  
A **fully autonomous trading bot** capable of making intelligent trade decisions, optimizing portfolio performance, and providing real-time insights through an interactive dashboard.
